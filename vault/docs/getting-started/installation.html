Installation & Setup

Get Jutis running in under five minutes. This guide walks you through installing the browser extension, setting up your wallet, and connecting to the Canton network.

Install the Jutis extension

Jutis is available for Chrome and Firefox. The browser extension installs locally and does not send your private keys or wallet data to any server.

info

Extension availability

The Jutis browser extension is not yet published to the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons. This documentation describes the intended installation experience once the extension is publicly available.

  1. Download for Chrome — Visit the Jutis website or the Chrome Web Store (link coming soon) and click Add to Chrome.
  2. Download for Firefox — Visit Firefox Add-ons (link coming soon) and click Add to Firefox.
  3. Pin the extension — After installation, click the puzzle piece icon in your browser's toolbar and pin Jutis for easy access.
  4. Verify the install — Click the Jutis icon in your toolbar. You should see the Jutis welcome screen.

Create or import a wallet

On first launch, Jutis prompts you to either create a new wallet or import an existing one using a recovery phrase.

Create a new wallet

Jutis generates a new cryptographic key pair locally on your device. The private key never leaves the extension. You'll be asked to set a password to lock your wallet locally.

key

Your recovery phrase (12 or 24 words) will be displayed on screen. Write it down immediately — this is the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access.

Import an existing wallet

If you already have a Canton wallet, enter your 12 or 24-word recovery phrase to import it into Jutis. The phrase is used locally to derive your private key — it is never transmitted to any server.

import_contacts

Importing a recovery phrase does not affect your existing wallet. Jutis derives a view of your existing keys — you still control your assets on the Canton network.

Backup your recovery phrase

Your recovery phrase is the most critical piece of data in your wallet. It can restore all your accounts and assets if the extension is deleted, the browser is reset, or the device fails.

warning

Never share your recovery phrase

Jutis staff will never ask for your recovery phrase. Anyone who has it can control your wallet and all assets. Store it offline — on paper or a hardware device — never in a note-taking app, cloud storage, or email.

Store your recovery phrase safely by following these rules:

  • Write it on paper — at least 2 copies, stored in separate physical locations
  • Keep it away from cameras, screens, and screenshot tools
  • Do not store it in any digital format (notes, photos, cloud, password managers)
  • Verify you can restore from the phrase before transferring real assets

Connect to the Canton network

Jutis is pre-configured to connect to the Canton network. No manual network configuration is required in the initial setup. Once installed, your wallet is ready to interact with Canton.

info

Canton network readiness

Jutis is architected for Canton. Full Canton testnet connectivity will be enabled in a future update. The extension is functional for local wallet management, but live Canton network operations are pending testnet activation.

To verify your connection setup:

  • Open the Jutis extension and check that your Canton address is displayed
  • Confirm that no error indicators appear on the network status
  • For testnet participation — watch the Jutis blog or Discord for testnet activation announcements

First transaction checklist

Before executing your first Canton asset transfer, verify each of the following to prevent accidental loss of funds.

check_circle

Recovery phrase is backed up and stored safely in at least two offline locations

check_circle

Wallet is secured with a strong local password in addition to the extension PIN

check_circle

You are connected to the correct Canton network (mainnet or testnet as intended)

check_circle

Recipient address is verified — copy and paste rather than typing manually

check_circle

Transaction amount and network fee are reviewed before confirming in the extension popup

check_circle

For first-time transfers, use a small test amount before sending larger balances
