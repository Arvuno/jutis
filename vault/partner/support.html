Frequently Asked Questions

When do I receive my referral rewards?
Rewards are approved after the referred user completes the required activity. You'll see "Reward Approved" in the Users tab, then it becomes available for withdrawal after the next payout cycle.
What's the minimum withdrawal amount?
The minimum withdrawal is $10.00 USD. Withdrawals are processed to your registered Canton wallet address.
How are rewards calculated?
Each campaign has its own reward value per qualified referral. You can view the reward rate when creating or editing a link in the Links tab.
Can I have multiple campaigns?
Yes — there's no limit on the number of campaigns or links you can create. We recommend using separate campaigns to track which channels drive the best results.
What happens if a user doesn't qualify?
If a referred user doesn't complete the required activity within the qualification window, they won't count as a qualified referral and no reward will be issued. You can still track their status in the Users tab.
How do I change my payout address?
Go to Settings → Payout Settings to update your Canton wallet address. Changes take effect on the next withdrawal request.
