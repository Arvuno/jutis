Security Architecture

Security built around your approval

Jutis is designed so that no transaction, no app connection, and no key access happens without your explicit, informed consent. Your keys stay on your device — always.

Non-Custodial Model

You own your keys. We own nothing.

Jutis is a non-custodial wallet interface. Your private keys are generated and stored locally — in your browser's secure storage, protected by your device's OS-level encryption. We never receive, store, or transmit your private keys at any point.

This is not a marketing claim — it's enforced by the architecture. There is no server-side key management, no cloud-based secret storage, and no recovery mechanism that gives Jutis access to your funds.

info

If you lose access to your device, Jutis cannot recover your keys. We strongly recommend setting up a recovery phrase and storing it safely offline.

Local Key Handling

Keys never leave your browser

The wallet extension communicates with your keys through the browser's secure context. No network request ever carries private key material.

01
vpn_key

Key Generation

Keys are generated locally using cryptographically secure random number generation in your browser's memory. The seed phrase is shown once — never stored by Jutis.

02
storage

Local Storage

Encrypted key storage uses your wallet password as the encryption key. The encrypted blob never leaves your device. Decryption happens only in memory, on demand.

03
lock

Signing In Memory

Transaction signing happens entirely in your browser's JavaScript context. The signed transaction — not the private key — is what gets sent to the Canton network.

Recovery & Backup

Your recovery path, your responsibility

Jutis is designed to be non-custodial, which means recovery depends entirely on you. We provide the tools — the safety of your funds depends on how you use them.

key

Recovery Phrase

Your 12 or 24-word recovery phrase is displayed once during setup. Write it down. Store it offline. Never share it. Jutis does not keep a copy.

cloud_upload

Encrypted Backup

An optional encrypted backup exports your keys in a password-protected format. The backup file alone is useless without your password.

hardware

Hardware Wallet

For the highest security, import or derive your keys on a hardware device. Jutis is designed to work with Ledger and Trezor devices.

Security Principles

How we think about security

01

User consent is mandatory

No action that affects your funds or permissions happens without your explicit, informed approval. There is no backdoor.

02

Least privilege by default

Apps get the minimum permissions they need — and nothing more. Read-only access is the default; explicit approval is required for anything beyond that.

03

Transparency over convenience

We show full transaction details before signing, even if it adds a step. Clarity matters more than speed.

04

Defense in depth

Multiple independent protections — browser sandboxing, encrypted storage, user approval gates — so no single failure compromises your funds.
